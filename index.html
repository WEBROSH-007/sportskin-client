About The Founder

Shaping India's Sportswear Future

Mridul Das, with 25+ years in sportswear, played a pivotal role at Future Group and Adidas India, driving a 10X growth in Adidas Kids & Golf. He led Khelo India's national sportswear innovation—impacting thousands of athletes.

In 2023, he launched Sportskin, a first-of-its-kind consultancy helping brands build performance wear through strategy, sustainability, and smart scaling.
